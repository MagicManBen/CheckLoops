Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CheckLoop.

How does CheckLoop help with CQC compliance?

+

CheckLoop provides a comprehensive framework for tracking and managing all aspects of CQC compliance. The system automatically monitors deadlines, sends alerts for upcoming requirements, generates reports for inspections, and provides a clear dashboard view of your practice's compliance status across all five key CQC domains.

Is CheckLoop suitable for practices of all sizes?

+

Absolutely! CheckLoop scales seamlessly from small single-GP practices to large multi-site healthcare organizations. The platform is designed to be flexible and can be configured to match your specific organizational structure and compliance requirements.

How secure is our practice data on CheckLoop?

+

Security is our top priority. CheckLoop uses enterprise-grade encryption, secure authentication systems, and is fully GDPR compliant. All data is stored in UK-based servers with regular security audits and penetration testing. We meet all NHS Digital Data Security and Protection Toolkit requirements.

Can CheckLoop integrate with our existing systems?

+

Yes, CheckLoop is designed with integration in mind. The platform can connect with common NHS and GP practice management systems, including EMIS Web, SystmOne, and Vision. Our API allows for custom integrations with other healthcare management systems if needed.

What kind of support do you provide?

+

We provide comprehensive support including initial setup assistance, staff training sessions, and ongoing technical support. Our UK-based support team is available Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm with emergency support available 24/7. We also provide regular system updates and feature enhancements based on user feedback.
