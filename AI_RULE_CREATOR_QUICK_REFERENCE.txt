💡 TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✅ DO:
  • Use exact names: "Dr Smith" not "smith"
  • Be specific: "wound check appointments" not "appointments"
  • Use clear operators: "at least 15 minutes" not "around 15 minutes"
  • Specify days: "weekdays" or "Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday"

❌ DON'T:
  • Be vague: "sometimes" or "usually"
  • Use abbreviations: "Dr S" instead of "Dr Smith"
  • Combine multiple rules in one sentence
